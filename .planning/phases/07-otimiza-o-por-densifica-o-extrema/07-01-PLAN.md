<objective>
Implement the density multiplier as the final step in the MCTS reward pipeline, rewarding compressed and logically-structured instructions over verbose chain-of-thought. Covers COGN-04: Densificação Extrema.

Purpose: The density multiplier acts as the final multiplicative factor on the consolidated MCTS score, boosting compressed outputs and penalizing verbosity. It operates in two layers: (1) a universal compression ratio based on len(child)/len(parent), and (2) a structured bonus for MutadorCognitivo outputs that contain the three cognitive headings.

Output: New `src/density_evaluator.py` module with `calculate_density_multiplier()` and `_has_structured_fields()`, 4 new config keys in `src/config.py`, density config loading + injection block in `src/optimizer.py`, and comprehensive test coverage.
</objective>
